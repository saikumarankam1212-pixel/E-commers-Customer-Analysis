# 🛒 E-Commerce Customer Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an E-Commerce customer dataset to uncover valuable business insights related to customer behavior, sales performance, product categories, payment methods, product returns, and customer churn. The project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow using **Python**, **SQL**, and **Power BI**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing product categories.
* Evaluate revenue and sales performance.
* Understand customer demographics and spending patterns.
* Analyze payment method preferences.
* Measure product return and customer churn rates.
* Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for business reporting.

# 📊 Key Business Insights

* Electronics generated the highest overall revenue.
* Customers aged 26–35 contributed the highest purchase value.
* Credit Card was the most preferred payment method.
* Female customers generated higher average revenue than male customers.
* Clothing products recorded the highest return rate.
* High-value customers contributed a significant share of total revenue.
* Product returns showed a relationship with customer churn.
* Product categories displayed different sales and profitability patterns.
* Revenue trends highlighted periods of higher customer demand.
* Customer purchasing behavior varied across product categories and payment methods.
